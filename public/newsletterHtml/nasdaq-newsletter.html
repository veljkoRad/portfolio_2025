Introduction

NASDAQ 100 and US election
 
The US election could influence the Nasdaq 100 in a couple of ways. Uncertainty regarding what the future political scene will look like usually increases volatility in periods before the election. During the latest election year in 2020, the Nasdaq 100 was up 46.85% with an increase of 12% in November alone, the month of the election.

Recent performance highlights
 
In 2023, the Nasdaq-100 had a remarkable annual performance, achieving a 55.1% total return—its best since 1999. Even though there were worries about a possible recession, the U.S. stock market managed to defy expectations and surprise analysts. So far, in 2024 Nasdaq 100 is up more than 6%, which continues to create new all-time highs.

NASDAQ 100 forward outlook
 
As for 2024, analysts remain cautiously optimistic. While gains may not match the substantial growth of 2023, there could be an upside potential of around 20% for the index’s top components. Factors behind this perspective include anticipations of interest rate reductions by the year's end, resilience in the consumer sector, and ongoing advancements within the tech industry such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and biotechnology.

Conclusion
 
The NASDAQ-100, led by industry giants such as Apple, Microsoft, and Nvidia, thrives on technological innovation. Despite concerns about a possible recession, the NASDAQ-100 performed well in 2023 and has continued to climb in 2024, reaching new highs.
